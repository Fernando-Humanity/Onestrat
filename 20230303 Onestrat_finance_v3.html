This is the proprietary strategic asset that compounds over time.

Anticipated Finance Questions
Q Why can't we build scenarios on top of existing spreadsheet data?
Because spreadsheets are unvalidated, fragmented, and static. When a stakeholder asked "Did you validate this slide?" about a MiStrat output, the answer was "I've never seen that slide in my life." That is a governance failure. The KB ensures every clinical assumption is validated before it enters any financial model. Scenarios built on spreadsheets are false forecasts — your PLO's exact concern.
Q How long until we see ROI?
Phase 1 alone reduces deal evaluation risk (~$200K savings per major deal) through auditable clinical assumptions. Phase 1.5 cuts PSW prep time by ~60% — measurable in the next cycle. Phase 2 compounds over years. Risk is phased and bounded. Value begins in month 6 and grows continuously.
Q What if executives don't trust LLM recommendations?
That is by design. We measure Human Agency Score (HAS) to ensure executives remain fully engaged, questioning, and in control. If executives stop questioning LLM recommendations, we intervene and reset the system. OneStrat enhances executive judgment — it does not replace it. Every recommendation is KB-grounded and auditable.
Q We already have Silk, Concierge, Evaluate Pharma — why build more?
OneStrat does not replace any of these tools — it connects them. Silk provides raw signals. Evaluate Pharma provides market data. Concierge provides conversational AI. None of them provides the structured cross-referencing, human validation layer, institutional memory, or coherent workflow that makes these signals actionable for strategic decisions. That gap costs Sanofi ~$800K in external consultants every two years. OneStrat closes it permanently.
Q What happens if we don't invest?
The current state continues: 90% of analyst time on manual collection, decisions made on unvalidated spreadsheet assumptions, PSW cycles rebuilt from scratch, institutional knowledge lost when people leave. Competitors with better intelligence infrastructure make faster, better-informed decisions. The $800K in external consultants becomes a recurring cost. And the proprietary ML asset — which takes 2-3 years to build — never starts compounding.
